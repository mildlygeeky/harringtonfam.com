Our Tiny Dancer

After attending weekly Saturday morning dance classes since last fall, Maggie’s first recital was finally here.  I was nervous as to how she would do – we had talked about going on stage and dancing with her friends, but I was pretty sure she had no idea what that actually entailed.  Rehearsal happened the day before and despite a little trepidation at first, she went out on stage and performed both her dances wonderfully!  Sunday’s performance included a costume change and having Daddy, Nana, Grandma, and Papa in the audience as well – and once again, she did great!  Enjoy below!

Tap routine

Ballet (this once was a little shakier…though they only spent about 10 minutes at the end of each class on ballet, and the distractions on stage are pretty entertaining!)

Despite a week of being unsure as to whether or not we (specifically I) would be able to even make the recital (see Graeme has arrived!) – I was so proud and excited to be able to cheer on our little girl who doesn’t seem so little these days!
