Goodbye, Summer

Summer of 2016 went by way too quickly! Being on maternity leave meant we were able to take advantage of a few more trips away and lots of mid-week playdates with friends – it was a great summer and while I’m glad for the cooler weather, not as excited to have to get back to real life!

Some summer highlights:

Graeme arrived…

Maggie turned 3…

We had an overnight in York, ME with the McArdles…

went to Plymouth with some Healeys…

  

spent a week in Brewster…

 

 

celebrated as Janelle and Jared tied the knot…

 

got in some cousin time…

and lots of fun with friends!

      

 

This was definitely a summer to remember, I’m sad to see it go!
