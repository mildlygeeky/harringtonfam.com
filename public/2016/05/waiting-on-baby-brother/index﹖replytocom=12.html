Waiting on baby brother

We’re less than 5 weeks from the due date for Baby Boy Harrington and we’re all getting ready.  Maggie loves to “listen” to my belly and tell me things like “the new baby says he wants to come out” or put her finger in my belly button and giggle that the baby is tickling her – it’s all really sweet and quite a change from her initial reaction!  As excited as she has become, I know she has NO idea what is really coming and how different life will be, but I know in the long run it’ll be worth the initial struggles to have a sibling, a friend for life.

Although I didn’t bother when pregnant with Maggie, we decided to do some maternity photos this time round – mainly because I wanted to capture some last photos before we became a family of 4.  We used Lyndsay Hannah Photography who I can’t say enough about! She is a friend of a friend who, despite only doing this for a short time professionally, is super talented – plus a really sweet person, which always helps!  Maggie had a blast and Elm Bank in Wellesley is going to be a new destination for us – their children’s garden was amazing.  Here are a few favorites!
