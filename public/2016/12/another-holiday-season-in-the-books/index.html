Another holiday season in the books

We celebrated Graeme’s 1st and Maggie’s 4th Christmas with no shortage of candy canes, caroling, and cheer! Maggie was really into Christmas this year and it made everything all the more exciting – from writing her list, to shopping for her brother and daddy, to posing for the holiday card photo, she did it all with a smile on her face and a skip in her step.  We got to meet Santa, see the Nutcracker her dance school put on, decorate gingerbread houses, and see lots of family and friends – while it’s always sad when the holidays are over, ours were so jam-packed that I’m looking forward to a quiet January!
