Edward M. Kennedy Institute Opening

So Pat ended up having an amazing opportunity to work on an app that would be used during the opening of the Edward M. Kennedy Institute for the U.S. Senate opening.  As part of the agreement, we got 2 passes to the opening ceremony.  With speakers including Elizabeth Warren and Barack Obama, who were we to say no?!

      

It was a fantastic day and, even though we had to watch from the adjacent tent, hearing from our political leaders was truly inspirational!

Plus – how could you not be proud of this?? EMK Institute for the U.S. Senate ceremonial signing – brought to you by Mildly Geeky
