Two.

Two. Years. How has it been two years??

What feels equally like a lifetime and a blink of an eye – Maggie turned two this month.  She has become quite the character over in the past year, and definitely in the last few months.  A chatty girl who loves all things purple, has a new obsession with dinosaurs, and has more “buddies” at school than we can name – she is growing into a little girl and is definitely no longer a baby.

A quick look at Maggie today:

Favorite songs: Baa Baa Black Sheep, Let It Go, ABCs
Loves to recite “5 Little Monkeys”
Stands at 34 inches and weighs 26 lbs – right in the middle of curve for her age on both.
Talking in short sentences – including “I do it myself” and “I like this ____ ”
Must have for sleep – Giraffe and Owl lovies (one of each) – thankfully we have 2 of each so they get washed biweekly!
Favorite new activity: letting mommy paint her toenails, purple of course.

We celebrated her birthday with the Harringtons/Healeys over the July 4th weekend, saw all of mommy’s side the day prior to her birthday at Aoife’s 1st birthday party, and had a special visit with the Blaisdell twins on her actual birthday.  3 different events with lots of cake, presents, and all our favorite people made for a great 2nd celebration!
