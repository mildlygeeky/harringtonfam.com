June 19, 2017

Happy happy birthday, Graeme!

And in the blink of an eye, Graeme has turned 1.

Our littlest has turned from a wrinkled little baby to a fearless, energetic, joyful toddler. Still in love with his big sister, Graeme tries his hardest to keep up with her, and it won’t be long until he does!  He’s days away from officially being a walker (has done up to 10 steps so far), attacks challenges with full force, and has a stubborn determination.  Some tidbits on Graeme today:

Teeth: 3 up top, 3 on the bottom
Favorite sayings: Ah and Da
Preferred language: Spanish (thank you Nuvia!)
Loves: all things with wheels, any protein-based foods, dancing to music, his best friend Mikey.
Personality: busy, stubborn, loving, and silly

We celebrated his birthday with family and friends, and the love and superpowers were in full force!
