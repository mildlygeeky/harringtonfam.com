June 18, 2018

Choo, choo, Graeme is 2!

Today Graeme turns 2.  He has quickly grown into an adventurous little boy who loves trains, balls, anything with wheels, and his mommy (much to daddy’s dismay at times).  He is sweet at times, aggressive at others, but always determined. Some stats on Graeme today:

Favorite songs: Thomas theme song, Itsy Bitsy Spider, ABCs
He’s heavier than Maggie was at this age, weighing in at 28lbs, but the same heigh at 34 inches. 
His vocabulary has been bursting in recent weeks – everything from “train table” to “Graeme do” to “Happy daddy day” for Father’s day to “Clay Buchholz” (when Pat asks who threw a no hitter!)

With our recent move, things have been hectic in Graeme’s life and he’s taken it all like a champ – new house, new bedroom, new school – all done without much fuss. He’s proven to be an easily adaptable kid – as long as there’s trains to distract him! We had a little party with some of his friends/cousins – of course, as it goes with 2nd children, only took a few photos!
