December 31, 2013

A Year in Review

This year has been a fantastic one for the Harringtons – here it is by the numbers!

30 years Patrick has been alive

12 months Mildly Geeky has been in full time activity and has already launched 18 websites!

11 different planes boarded and 5 places visited (some multiple times) – Phoenix, DC, San Francisco, Chatham, and Moultonborough

6 foods Maggie will now eat (many of them her Papa Jim won’t even touch!) – Butternut squash, Sweet Potatoes, Apples, Pears, Bananas, Avocado

4 family members added this year (by birth) Aubrey Jordan, Charlotte Fallon, Maggie and (by marriage) Diana Harrington.

3rd anniversary of our wedding celebrated with a baby-free night out

2 weddings attended – congrats to Timmy and Diana Harrington and Steven and Diane Vetree!

 

1 very special hospital stay!

2013 was one for the books – looking forward to what 2014 will bring, Happy New Year!
