Joys of home ownership

So far we’ve been really lucky with our house – minimal work needed when we moved in, only minor issues so far – until recently.  We had been struggling with our heating system and not getting great pressure.  The Saturday before Thanksgiving, Pat and Cyril decided to see if they could get the pressure back up and with some fiddling they did….and it started raining in the dining room!  Turns out we had a hole in a pipe that had been slowly corroding for possibly years.

This lovely little leak led to a giant hole in our dining room ceiling, all the furniture being moved to the living room, and us being stuck with only 2 rooms of living space for a few weeks.

Thankfully right before this incident we had gotten Maggie a new table and chairs for her to color at – which made a great substitute dining set, albeit a little low to the ground!

 

As of now things are back to normal – just in time to start decorating for Christmas!!
