Fall Fun

Fall has always been my favorite season – give me anything and everything you can with apples, pumpkin, or cinnamon and I’m happy!  As with past years, we took full advantage of the weather and lots of seasonal activities…

…we picked apples.

….and played with pumpkins.

   

…and had playdates with lots of friends.

….and enjoyed a weekend in Maine for Tim and Amanda’s wedding!

 

and had a very Happy Halloween!

Other life updates – I started a new job in September as Sales & Marketing Program Manager at Medullan, it’s been an exciting and busy transition so far, but I’m loving it!  Pat is continually busy with clients and Maggie is growing up so much lately – getting more and more personality and becoming more independent every day.

As the weather gets colder we’ll be sad for the reduction in outdoor activities – thankfully we have the holidays to look forward to!
